Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 11, 2026

Todos for Everyone ("the App") is a personal task-management application offered by WebSurfer ("we", "us"). This policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and the choices you have.

What we collect

  • Account information — your email address, plus an optional first name, last name, and profile picture you provide. If you sign in with Apple, Google, or Facebook, we receive only the basic profile fields that provider returns (name and email).
  • App content — the todos, categories, and preferences you create inside the App. This data is stored in your account so it can sync across your devices.
  • Crash and diagnostic data — anonymized error reports (stack traces, device model, OS version) used to fix bugs. These reports do not include the contents of your todos.

How we use it

  • To authenticate you and let you sign in across devices.
  • To store and sync your todos, categories, and profile.
  • To diagnose and fix crashes and bugs.

We do not sell your data, share it with advertisers, or use it for advertising or marketing.

Third-party services

The App uses the following processors strictly to deliver the functionality described above:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Crashlytics, Hosting) for sign-in, data storage, and crash reporting. See the Firebase Privacy and Security page.
  • Apple Sign in with Apple, Google Sign-In, and Facebook Login — used only when you choose those sign-in methods. Each is governed by its provider's own privacy policy.

Data retention and deletion

Your account data is kept until you delete it. You can delete your account and all associated todos, categories, and profile information at any time from inside the App: open the Profile panel and tap Delete account. Once initiated, deletion is immediate and permanent.

Children

The App is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Security

Account data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored in Firestore with server-enforced rules that restrict each user's data to their own account. No system is perfectly secure, but we follow industry-standard practices.

Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above; continued use of the App after a change constitutes acceptance.
